Our Queens

Meet our beautiful furless girls!

All of our queens are fully health-tested and spend all of their time indoors with us. They are all part of the family and are, first and foremost, family pets. They all receive love and attention, which in turn creates a great temper and makes them very social. Visit their pages to get to know them and to see some cute pictures of them and their past kittens!

All of our adult cats are health tested clear for CMS, BHP, FIV/FELV, HCM genes as well as receiving an annual echocardiogram (heart scan).
