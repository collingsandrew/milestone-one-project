FAQ

If you are looking for a sociable and affectionate feline companion, the Sphynx may be the perfect breed for you. Always seeking out the cosiest spot in your home and often taking over your bed, a Sphynx will always want to be around you and love nothing more than a cuddle from their human. They are happiest if they are not left alone for long periods of time, so they best suit a lifestyle where you can be home most of the time. If you are prepared to spend a little more time than your average cat on bathing, nail trims, and ear cleaning, you will be rewarded with the most loving companion.

Sphynx do require a little more care than your average domestic cat. Generally, we recommend bathing frequency be assessed on an individual basis and done as and when you feel necessary for your cat. We do not recommend bathing more than once per week, but we find most of our cats need bathed monthly. Ear cleaning and nail trimming should be done on a weekly basis, however, and all of our kittens are used to this from a young age.
